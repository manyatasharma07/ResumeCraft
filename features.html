What is ResumeCraft?

ResumeCraft is a simple and powerful resume builder that helps you create clean, modern and creative resumes without any design skills. You can choose between Professional, Balanced and Funky styles depending on your needs.

Resume Styles

Best for jobs, internships and formal applications.

Balanced

A mix of modern design and professionalism.

Creative, colorful and eye-catching designs.
